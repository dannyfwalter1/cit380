Flexibility

flexibility is the ability to move through an unrestricted and pain free range of motion. Living a sedentary life style causes us to lose flexibility. Often, loss of flexibility or range of motion is not only not ideal, but can also lead to injury.

Joints requre movement through a full range of motion to maintain healthy joint structure. Considerin most individuals work or study sitting down at a desk, we lose a lot of mobility in our hips, back, shoulders and knees. Muscles that lack flexibility tire quickly and cause opposing muscle groups to work harder. Quad-hamstring, chest-back, and left and right imbalances all can be detrimental to our physical health.

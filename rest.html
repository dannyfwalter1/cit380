Rest

We aren't getting enough sleep. According to the CDC, one in three adults don't get enough sleep. The average adult needs seven to nine hours of sleep. Sleep has several benefits:

  • Improve your immune system
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Decrease risk for health issues (diabetes and heart disease)
  • Reduce stress and improve mood
  • Be more alert during the day
  • Repair muscles

Some things to help improve your quality of sleep are:

  • Going to bed at the same time everyday
  • Waking up at the same time everyday
  • Turning off distractions
  • Avoid large meals and caffeinated beverages before bed

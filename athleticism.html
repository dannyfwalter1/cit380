Athleticism

Athleticism is sort of like a catch all for several other components of fitness. These include: motor skills, agility, balance, and coordination. These exercises are incredibly important not because it transforms you into an athlete but is another important aspect in leading a quality life and avoiding injury.

Motor skills can be improved by performing the following exercises:

  • Crawling
  • Throwing
  • Catching
  • Climbing

Agility skills can be improved by performing the following exercises:

  • Jumping
  • Shuttle runs
  • Ladder drills
  • Agility hurdles

Balance skills can be improved by performing the following exercises:

  • Single leg exercises (squat and deadlift)
  • Beam walks
  • Yoga
  • Tai Chi

Coordination skills can be improved by performing the following exercises:

  • Walking lunges
  • Dribbling a soccer or basketball
  • Catching and throwing
  • Jumping rope
